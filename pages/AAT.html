Aviation & Transportation

DKS DESIGN played a vital role in the design and documentation of multiple fast-food outlets located within the New Orleans Airport. This large-scale project brought together a diverse range of well-known food and beverage brands under one roof, enhancing the passenger experience through thoughtfully designed commercial spaces.

New Orleans Airport

Our scope included:

  • Creating accurate and coordinated BIM models for over 45 outlets
  • Delivering detailed construction documents for build-out and compliance
  • Working closely with brand-specific guidelines to maintain identity and consistency

Some of the leading outlets in this project included:

  • Starbucks
  • Smoothie King
  • McDonald's
  • And many more national and regional chains

This project reflects DKS DESIGN'S capability to manage multi-unit commercial projects with speed, precision, and consistency—making us a reliable partner for high-traffic public spaces like airports.
