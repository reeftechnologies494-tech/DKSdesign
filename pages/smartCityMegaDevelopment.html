Smart City & Mega Developments

DKS DESIGN was entrusted with a highly technical and collaborative role in the development of a Precast Yard facility located in NEOM City, Saudi Arabia. Our team was responsible for delivering detailed and fully coordinated BIM models, along with MEP construction documentation to support this large-scale infrastructure initiative.

NEOM City, Saudi Arabia

Our contributions included:

  • Creation of a complete and coordinated MEP BIM model
  • Development of the federated model combining all disciplines for seamless coordination
  • Generation of a detailed clash detection report to ensure early issue resolution and efficient site execution
  • Implementation of unique clash codes across the model to streamline communication and issue tracking

This project reflects DKS DESIGN capability to handle industrial and infrastructure-level BIM scopes with a high degree of technical accuracy, collaboration, and project control—especially in visionary developments like NEOM.
