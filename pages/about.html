Design Intent & Site Reality

At DKS Design, we transform complex architectural visions into coordinated, constructible data. By leveraging advanced BIM workflows, we eliminate field conflicts before they occur, ensuring every project is engineered for precision and seamless execution. We specialize in comprehensive multi-disciplinary coordination, acting as the vital link between conceptual intent and real-world construction.

With over a decade of expertise and a portfolio covering millions of square feet, our team delivers technically accurate BIM models, fully coordinated MEPF systems, and fabrication-ready documentation. Our process is engineered to minimize waste, optimize site logistics, and guarantee reliability.

Digital Design&ENGINEERING STACK

AutoCAD

Used for precise 2D drafting and foundational layout designs, ensuring a solid base for architectural and MEPF engineering workflows.

Tekla

Specialized tool for constructible, high-detail structural steel and concrete modeling, enabling seamless fabrication and erection.

Advance Steel

Comprehensive 3D modeling and detailing essential for accelerating steel engineering and automating fabrication processes.

Navisworks

Advanced project review platform used for immersive 3D coordination, intelligent clash detection, and construction simulation.

SketchUp

Intuitive 3D modeling software favored for rapid conceptual framing, spatial visualization, and exploring design iterations.

3ds Max

The industry standard for producing high-fidelity 3D rendering, detailed texturing, and photorealistic architectural visualizations.

Revit

Parametric 3D modeling software used to design, model, and document buildings and infrastructure with intelligent, model-based information.
