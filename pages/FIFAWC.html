Sports and Recreational Infrastructure

DKS DESIGN was proud to be part of the globally celebrated FIFA World Cup project in Qatar, contributing to the design and documentation of a series of ornamentally designed kiosks and public amenities. The project featured five unique types of kiosks, along with walkways and toilet blocks, all crafted with a high level of design detail and cultural relevance.

FIFA World Cup, Qatar

Our contributions included:

  • Developing accurate BIM models for each kiosk and amenity structure
  • Providing construction documentation to support fabrication and on-site execution
  • Ensuring all designs aligned with the architectural vision and functional requirements of the event
  • Supporting design development for intricate architectural elements and finishes

These structures were not only functional but also visually iconic, enhancing the overall aesthetic and experience for visitors during the FIFA World Cup. DKS DESIGN’S involvement ensured precision, beauty, and reliability across all units delivered.
