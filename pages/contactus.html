Frequently Asked Questions

What information do you need to start a BIM project?
We typically require the architectural blueprints, structural drawings, project standards (if any), and the desired Level of Development (LOD). Once received, we'll provide a comprehensive proposal.
Do you offer on-site deployment or just remote services?
Both! DKS Design offers robust secondment services where our engineering and BIM professionals can be deployed directly to your project site or work seamlessly from our remote hub.
How fast can you deliver MEPF coordination?
Timelines depend entirely on project scale and complexity. However, our optimized workflows ensure rapid clash-detection turnarounds, keeping you ahead of the construction schedule. Contact us to discuss your specific timeline.
